Monitoring System for Collaborative Rain Gauge Stations

This website aims to improve the efficiency and service quality of climatological instruments, specifically for observatory-type collaborative rain gauge stations. Through a web-based platform, users can easily access and manage metadata, observer ratings, equipment condition, and condition risk matrices in a more transparent way.

Visit the website

Main Dashboard Page:

The dashboard presents a summary of the overall condition of collaborative rain gauge stations. It includes colored markers to show the distribution of equipment condition status across the Aceh province map.

Condition Details Page:

This interactive sidetab appears when a marker is clicked. It displays detailed information for each collaborative rain gauge station and is designed to assist in managing and monitoring the condition of various equipment. The tab also indicates the damage level—for example, identifying instruments that fall under the “Severely Damaged” category.

Risk Matrix and Recommendation Page:

This sidetab helps identify, analyze, and prioritize equipment-related risks based on collected data. It also provides actionable recommendations. With this matrix, equipment managers can make more informed decisions for efficient and structured maintenance or repairs.

Metadata Page for Collaborative Rain Gauge Stations:

This metadata tab is designed to improve the ease of accessing detailed metadata for each station. With proper optimization, this page serves as an efficient and reliable archive for equipment metadata information.
